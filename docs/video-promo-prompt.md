Use this prompt with **Sora, Kling AI, Runway Gen-3, or Pika**. Two versions are provided: a single long-form prompt for AI video generators, and a scene-by-scene breakdown for human video editors / storyboarders.

## Version 1 — Single AI Video Generator Prompt (60 seconds)

> Paste this directly into Sora / Kling / Runway prompt box.

```
A cinematic 60-second promotional video for Renovaara, an AI-powered beauty analysis app. The visual language is soft luxury — warm pink and violet gradients, glowing UI elements, dreamy bokeh, and gentle slow-motion footage of a confident South Asian woman in her late 20s.

SCENE 1 (0–4s): Opening. A close-up of a woman's hand unlocking her phone. The screen glows pink. Soft ambient music fades in. Gentle lens flare. Black background with the word "Renovaara" appearing in elegant serif lettering (Lora font), followed by a pink-to-violet gradient underline that animates in.

SCENE 2 (4–10s): She holds her phone up and takes a selfie in soft morning window light. The camera is front-facing. She smiles naturally. Her surroundings are minimal — white wall, morning light. The shot is warm and intimate, like she is doing something personal and exciting.

SCENE 3 (10–18s): The screen transitions to a stylized UI mockup of the Renovaara analysis loading screen. Glowing pink and violet particles swirl around a face silhouette. Text appears word by word: "Analyzing face shape... color season... skin type..." Beautiful data-visualization aesthetic. A progress ring completes.

SCENE 4 (18–27s): Quick-cut montage of four report cards appearing on screen, each one sliding up with a soft spring animation:
  — A "Face Shape" card showing "Oval" with illustrated face silhouettes
  — A "Color Season" card showing "Soft Autumn — Warm Undertone" with a swatch palette in terracotta, rust, and olive tones
  — A "Skin Routine" card showing AM/PM steps with ingredient notes
  — A "Style Guide" card showing silhouette illustrations labeled "Wear" and "Avoid"
Each card has a soft pink glow, white background, and Lora serif headings.

SCENE 5 (27–37s): The AI Makeup Studio. Split-screen: left side shows the woman's real face; right side shows the virtual makeup applied — a warm berry lip, soft peach blush, subtle bronzed contour. She tilts her head, smiling at the change. The text "Try before you buy" fades in in elegant Raleway font, violet-colored.

SCENE 6 (37–44s): Hairstyle virtual try-on. Same woman, now with a short lob layered cut rendered on her. She touches her hair and laughs softly. Text: "Haircut confidence. Finally." Next cut: glasses frame try-on — a tortoiseshell cat-eye frame overlaid on her face. Text: "Find frames that actually fit your face."

SCENE 7 (44–52s): The full report view — a phone held vertically showing a scrolling beauty report. Every section glows softly as the camera slowly zooms toward the screen. Voiceover (warm female voice, Indian accent): "Your face. Your season. Your complete style blueprint — ready in under 60 seconds."

SCENE 8 (52–57s): Social proof moment. Three floating testimonial cards appear with soft drop shadows:
  — "I stopped buying the wrong foundations." — Meera R.
  — "I changed my haircut confidently." — Emma L.
  — "Finally, colors that actually suit me." — Sarah K.
Cards appear one after another with a gentle stagger.

SCENE 9 (57–60s): Closing brand card. Full-screen soft pink gradient (#FDF2F8 to white). The Renovaara logo appears centered in pink (#EC4899). Below it: "Your free analysis awaits." A violet (#8B5CF6) pill button appears: "Try Free — No Card Needed". The woman from the video appears in the bottom corner, smiling, holding her phone. Fade to white.

VISUAL STYLE:
- Color palette: Soft pink (#EC4899), violet (#8B5CF6), warm white (#FDF2F8), dark navy (#0a0a0f) for text
- Lighting: Warm, soft, golden-hour or morning diffused light. No harsh shadows.
- Camera movement: Slow push-ins, gentle handheld warmth, elegant parallax on UI screens
- Typography on screen: Lora serif for headings, Raleway sans-serif for body text
- UI mockups: iPhone 15 Pro frame, screen recordings with smooth transitions
- Mood: Empowering, luxurious, intimate, modern. Think skincare ad meets Apple product launch.
- Aspect ratio: 9:16 for Reels/TikTok; also export 16:9 for YouTube

AUDIO:
- Background music: Soft lo-fi piano with a gentle beat drop at the makeup studio scene (27s). Build through the testimonials. End on a sustained chord.
- Voiceover (optional): Warm, confident Indian female voice, measured pace, 3–4 lines maximum.
- No jarring sound effects. Every transition has a soft whoosh or shimmer.
```

### Critical: Aspect Ratio Must Be Set via API Parameter

Seedance **ignores aspect ratio in the prompt text**. Set it via the Replicate API input:

```json
{ "aspect_ratio": "9:16" }   // for Reels / TikTok / Shorts
{ "aspect_ratio": "16:9" }   // for YouTube / website hero (default — what generated the first video)
{ "aspect_ratio": "1:1" }    // for Instagram feed / square posts
```

The "vertical 9:16" phrase in the prompt text has no effect — it is always the API parameter that controls output orientation.
