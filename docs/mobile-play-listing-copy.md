## Full description (Play Console)

Renovaara helps you preview and plan your next look before you decide.

Start with one selfie and use AI Studio to try makeup styles, hair color options, glasses looks, and outfit ideas. When you want deeper guidance, unlock your complete beauty report with face shape, color analysis, skin insights, and personalized recommendations.

What you can do in Renovaara:
- Try AI makeup and hair looks from your own photo
- Generate glasses and outfit suggestions that match your profile
- Create and manage a personal style canvas
- Save favorite outputs in your Studio vault
- Access your report insights and continue your style journey

Why users choose Renovaara:
- Fast look experimentation before real-world decisions
- One app for try-ons, guidance, and report history
- Personalized recommendations based on your profile

Privacy and control:
- Sign in securely to access your reports and studio tools
- Camera and gallery access are used only for photo-based features
- You can manage your generated looks and account data

Important note:
Renovaara provides style and beauty guidance only. It is not a medical or diagnostic service.
